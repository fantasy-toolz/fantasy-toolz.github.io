Upcoming MLB Game Predictions

Predictions for the next week of MLB games, updated daily. The predictions are based on the FantasyToolz run differential differential model. This model looks at recent performance of teams. The associated calculations are fully available in the associated GitHub repository. Be warned that at present this model does not take pitching matchups into account.

The quality of the game is the mean run differential of the two teams. Positive numbers indicate the two teams are on average better than a typical matchup.

To track how the predictions have done over the season, see this image. The figure bins the games into predicted win percentages, and then looks at the fraction of those games that were won. If the red line goes through the points, the predictions are doing (statistically) well.
